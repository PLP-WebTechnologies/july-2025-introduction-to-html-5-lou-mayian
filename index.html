KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP

This Kakuma was formed in 1992 with majority of the refugees coming from South Sudanese following Anyanya 2 war between Sudan Army and the people of the south's rebels.

SERVICES GIVEN TO REFUGEES BY UNHCR

The following services are offered at Kakuma

  1. Education
  2. food
  3. scholarship
  4. loans
  5. shelter
